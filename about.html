About Kumanyini Blocks

We provide reliable concrete block manufacturing for homes and commercial projects across Bono East and beyond.

Our Story

Kumanyini Blocks was founded in Asekye Nkoranza with a mission to provide durable, trusted blocks for Ghanaian construction projects. We focus on consistency, reliability, and practical pricing for every customer.

Milestones

  • 2018: Founded in Asekye Nkoranza
  • 2020: Expanded to 8-inch block production
  • 2022: Serving all of Bono East Region
  • 2024: Added paving block line
